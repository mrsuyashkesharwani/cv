Important Points of the Insurance:

  • Death Benefit:
    In case of Death during the policy term 125% of Sum Assured plus Reversionary Bonus plus Final additional bonus (if any) is given. However, if Death occurs after completion of policy then only Basic Sum Assured is paid. Death Benefit is paid only if all the due Premiums have been paid.
  • Maturity Benefits
    When term of the policy is over and all the premiums have been paid, Amount of Sum Assured + Reversionaly Bonus + Final Additional Bonus is paid in lumpsum.
    Participation in Profits is a concept where in LIC distributes its profits to its shareholder through different types of bonuses. Since, Jeevan Anand is a participating policy so bonuses are paid in this policy too.
  • Optional Benefits:
    Accidental Death Rider: If you avail this additional benefit then in case of death because of accident, Nominee get additional amount plus Sum Assured. Disablity Rider: If you avail this additional benefit then in case person becomes disable because of mishappening then he gets Amount of insurance even while he is alive.
  • Disability Benefit Rider:
    Under this Benefit, in case Policy holder becomes disable then future premiums of the policy are waived off but policy remains in force and you get benefits like Death Benefit and Maturity Benefit without paying premiums.
